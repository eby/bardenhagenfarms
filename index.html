About the Farm

We have delighted in growing the highest quality, tasty fruits for over a century. Nothing makes us happier than sharing our bounty with people who love fresh-picked fruits.

We grow apples & seedless table grapes. We are not a U-pick farm, but we do have a farm stand for you to visit during the summer. We sometimes have apricots and peaches, plums and nectarines at our farm stand as well — so stop by!

You'll also find our produce at local grocery stores and farm markets in the Northwest Michigan area and through our local food co-op (MI Farm Co-op).

Follow us on Facebook for the latest on what's available this season.
